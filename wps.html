Goal

We will discuss weighted projective spaces (and weighted projective varieties), and their relationship to stacks.

Topics that we intend to cover:

  • local structure of weighted projective spaces
  • varieties in weighted projective spaces
  • weighted projective lines (which are not weighted projective spaces, despite their name)
